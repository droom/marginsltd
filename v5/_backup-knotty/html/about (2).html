About.

26
BRISTOL

I've had an interest in Motion Graphics since I saw the menu for the Namco video game 'Ridge Racer Type 4' in 1999. I spent more time admiring that design than playing the game. This early interest has given me a great understanding of movement, composition and narrative.

I've previously worked as a HTML Designer, progressing to Flash designing to incorporate my love for movement. I've spent the last few years learning After Effects, in which I have rapidly progressed. I'm currently learning Cinema 4D. I think this range of ability is my strongest asset.

Some people have asked me if this site is a template. I'd like to mention it very much is not!
